CanVol

We bring passionate volunteers of all ages together with local charities that make a difference.

How does CanVol work?

Find a charity

Search for charities in your area by your age and interests. Read unfiltered reviews by your peers about their experiences volunteering at the charity.

Volunteer! Make a difference

Find volunteer openings for the charity or contact them directly. Start volunteering, and make a difference in the lives of Calgarians.

Rate and review

Share your volunteering experience with the Charity. Tell others of your experience, helping your peers find good places to help and gain experience, and places to avoid!

We're Calgarian!

CanVol is for Calgary. We are forming great relationships with Calgary-based charities. If your a Calgarian charity, we can send you an advanced day-one ready package.
